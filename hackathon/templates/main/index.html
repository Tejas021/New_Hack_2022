About VCET HACKATHON

VCET Hackathon is a 30 hours Virtual Hackathon organized by Department of Information Technology of "Vidyavardhini's College of Engineering and Technology". By organizing this Hackathon, we are trying to promote a strong Programming and Product Building Culture among students that will help them develop Problem Solving, Critical Thinking and Software Development Skills. It is an opportunity to take on challenging problems that revolve around us all the time and crack them down.

Will you get time to eat?     Can you ditch your sleep?    Do you have passion to build?   
Find out in this 30 hours long thrilling experience!

Event Guideline

RULES

  • The VCET Hackathon will take place on 1st & 2nd October 2021.
  • A team can consist of 1 to 4 members. Every member of the team must be a student of a university or a college. A team can have members from different colleges.
  • Entry fees is Rs.500 per team.
  • Participants are free to use open standard repositories, APIs and publically available libraries.
  • Progress of the project will be tracked during Hackathon. Use of readymade code or precompiled code will lead to disqualification.
  • Participants are expected to behave professionally and responsibly.
  • Decisions made by organizers and judges are final.
  • Participants will have to submit abstracts for all five problem statements.
  • All updates regarding the event will be posted on Social Media handles as well as mailed to each team.

This was the first time that VCET was organising Hackathon and which went on to be one of the most successful events of the college. In a nutshell, VCET HACKATHON’2021 proved to be another feather in our cap.

FAQ?

What is the mode of the Hackathon?
VCET Hackathon’21 Reloaded will be held online.
Who can participate?
Anyone is welcome to participate in this event. While experienced coding and programming is a huge plus, teams will also need people with strong presentation skills and unique ideas.
Can a pass out student participate in the event?
No, every member of the team must be a current student of a university or a college. (Members from different colleges allowed).
What are the Problems Statements that can be selected?
Problem Statements will be provided from our end. You have to submit abstracts for all 5 Problem Statements, out of which 1 will be allocated.
How much is the entry fee and the last date for registration?
Entry fee is 500/- per team and the last date of registration is September 19, 2021.
Which payment methods do you accept?
Online payments via NEFT/UPI will be accepted.
Will a dataset be provided?
No, the data has to be collected on your own.
What is the selection criteria?
Innovation, Technology, Completion & Business Values are some of the criterias.
On which platform will the event be conducted?
Google Meet, YouTube live streaming and Discord.
When and where will the final results be announced?
Results will be announced at the end of the Final Pitching round. (Google Meet & YouTube live stream)
What is the cancellation policy like?
There is no cancellation policy and payment once done will not be refunded.
Having another questions that is not answered in the FAQs?
You can contact us via mail or drop your query in the comment sections on our Social Media handles.
